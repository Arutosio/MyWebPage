Kanji Learning Tool - FAQ

This tool helps you memorize Kanji readings (Kun Yomi & On Yomi) through interactive exercises.
  • Select a Kanji list from the dropdown menu.
  • Click "Add" to include the Kanji set in your learning session.
  • With KunYomi or OnYomi buttons you can enable or disable them.
  • Press "Start/Stop" to begin or end a practice session.
  • Type the correct Kun Yomi or On Yomi in the input fields and press Enter or click Check.
  • The progress bar and the icons in the center tracks your learning progress.

Learning Kanji can be difficult, especially when trying to memorize different readings. This tool provides an interactive way to reinforce knowledge through repetition and testing.

It is designed for those who want a structured yet flexible way to study Kanji, track progress, and focus on Kun Yomi and On Yomi pronunciations efficiently.
